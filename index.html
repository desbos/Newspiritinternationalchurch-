meet the founder

The Founder of Newspirit Int'l Church Owerri is Pastor Chike Williams Nwokeocha, from Abia state. He is married to Pastor Favour ChikeWilliams.

The vision of Newspirit Int'l Church was given to the founder during his 200l in the university, Abia State University Uturu Nigeria in the year 2007

Pastor Chike Williams.N. was directed by the leading of the Holy spirit and confirmed by book Of Joel 2:28-30, to start the ministry from the campuses.

Newspirit Int'l Church had her first inauguration for the campus phase at Alvan Ikoku Federal College of Education Owerri, Imo state on 7th of December , 2014.
